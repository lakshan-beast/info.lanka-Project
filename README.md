# Info.Lanka – Sri Lanka Public Services Porta

<div>
  <h3>Info.Lanka – Sri Lanka Public Services Portal</h3>
  <p>
    Info.Lanka is a fully responsive, centralized web portal designed to provide 
comprehensive information on government services across Sri Lanka. The platform enables users to quickly access service details, official links, and guidance for various ministries, departments, and public institutions, all in one place.
  </p>
  
  <h4>Technologies Used:</h4>
  <ul>
    <li>HTML</li>
    <li>CSS</li>
    <li>JavaScript</li>
  </ul>

  <h4>Key Features:</h4>
  <ul>
    <li>Service Categories & Cards: Organized main & sub-services with short descriptions and official links for quick access.</li>
    <li>Search & Filter Functionality: Real-time search with keyword and tag-based filtering for easy navigation.</li>
    <li>FAQ Section: Answers to common questions about government procedures and portal usage.</li>
    <li>Legal & Support Pages: Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, Disclaimer, and Report Incorrect Info sections for transparency and trust.</li>
    <li>Interactive Notes & Chat: Add love notes and communicate privately within the portal.</li>
    <li>Responsive Design: Works seamlessly across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.</li>
    <li>User-Friendly UI: Clean, modern interface with cards, buttons, and clear visual hierarchy.</li>
  </ul>

  <h4>Challenges & Learnings:</h4>
  <ul>
    <li>Learned to structure a multi-level service portal with main categories, sub-services, and dynamic content.</li>
    <li>Practiced real-time search functionality and array/object handling in JavaScript.</li>
    <li>Enhanced skills in building responsive, accessible, and professional UI/UX suitable for a public-facing platform.</li>
    <li>Learned best practices for presenting legal, privacy, and support information professionally.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
